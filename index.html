My Story

I consider myself a life-long student that is driven by a passion for learning. Formally a pre-medical student, I attended Southern Utah University for my undergraduate education. Following graduation, I transitioned into the quality industry for medical devices but that drive for learning and growth persisted. Returning to my alma mater, I enrolled in an MBA program to increase and diversify my knowledge base. Fast-foward to the year 2022, I was introduced to my most exciting venture yet: software development.

After attending a coding Bootcamp at Devmountain, I've been educated on full-stack engineering and found myself being the most passionate I've been to learn. This passion for learning, positive attitude and a life-long student menality has led me to software development. I believe those traits will also help me be successful on this career path and I cannot wait to get started.
